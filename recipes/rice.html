My Favorite Persian Rice Recipe

Description

The most delicisious persian rice that you will ever try,
this home cooked meal takes its inspiration from a greek dish
with ground black pepper, garlic and onions, and other spices.
This is a dish that you will wants to service to please every rice lower's heart

So How Do We Make this Delicious Rice?

It only takes 30 minutes to impress your date with this mediterian delicacy

  • Step 1:
    • Place water and kosher salt in a pot; bring to a boil.
    • Add rice; cook and stir for exactly 7 minutes.
    • Drain.

  • Step 2:
    • Heat olive oil in a pot over medium-high heat.
    • Cover the bottom of the pot with 1 layer of potato slices.
    • Sprinkle cumin and salt over potatoes. Cook until potatoes are sizzling, 2 to 3 minutes;
      top potatoes with rice to form an even layer.
    • Reduce heat to low and place butter slices over rice.

  • Step 3:
    • Top the pot with a layer of clean paper towels, then place the lid over the towels.
    • Steam until rice is fluffy, about 45 minutes..

  • Step 4:
    • Grind saffron threads with a mortar and pestle.
    • Mix crushed saffron with 1 1/2 tablespoons hot water in a large bowl.
    • Add a couple of spoonfuls of rice to saffron mixture and stir until rice is yellow.

  • Step 5:
    • Spoon remaining rice into a serving bowl.
    • Top with saffron rice and line the edges of the bowl with potatoes.
    • Garnish with parsley..

Ingredients

  • 3 quarts of water
  • 3 tablespoons kosher salt
  • 2 cups basmati rice, rinsed
  • 1 russet potato, cut into 1/4-inch slices
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
