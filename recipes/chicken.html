My Favorite Chicken Recipe

Description

This flavorful Southern-fried chicken is seasoned with paprika, salt,
ground black pepper, garlic and onion powders, and cayenne pepper.

So How Do We Make this Amazing Chicken?

You will find the full, step-by-step recipe below â€” but here is a
brief overview of what you can expect when you make Southern-fried chicken:

  1. Combine the flour and seasonings in a zip-top bag.
  2. Add the chicken to the bag and toss to coat.
  3. Chill the floured chicken in the fridge.
  4. Toss the chilled, floured chicken in the flour mixture one more time.
  5. Fry the chicken until an instant read thermometer reads 165 degrees F.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 (3 pound) whole chicken, cut into pieces(Optional)
  • vegetable oil for frying
  • salt to taste
